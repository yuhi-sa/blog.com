date: 2024-10-28T09:10:00+09:00

tags: ["Shell"]
description: "A simple shell script to find Homebrew packages that are not depended on by any other installed packages, helping you identify candidates for removal."
---

<!--more-->

```.sh
for pkg in $(brew list); do
  if [ -z "$(brew uses --installed $pkg)" ]; then
    echo "$pkg can be uninstalled"
  fi
done
```
