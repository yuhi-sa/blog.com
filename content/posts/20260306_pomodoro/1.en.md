## What is the Pomodoro Technique?

The Pomodoro Technique alternates 25-minute focused work sessions with 5-minute breaks to maintain concentration throughout the day.

## Features

[Pomodoro Timer](https://yuhi-sa.github.io/pomodoro/) is a free browser-based timer with:

- **Circle Progress Timer** - Visual SVG-based countdown with color-coded phases
- **Work Time Tracking** - Automatic recording of sessions with daily totals and 7-day charts
- **Picture-in-Picture** - Float the timer above other apps while working
- **Sound Notifications** - Beep alerts when sessions end (toggleable)
- **Session History** - Detailed log with timestamps and durations
- **Data Persistence** - History saved in localStorage across sessions
- **Responsive Design** - Works on desktop and mobile

## How to Use

1. Visit [Pomodoro Timer](https://yuhi-sa.github.io/pomodoro/)
2. Tap/click to start a work session
3. Focus for 25 minutes
4. Take a 5-minute break
5. Repeat
